Welcome to MedTrack

Privacy First

All your medication data is stored locally on your device. We don't have servers, accounts, or tracking.

Important: Your data can be lost if you clear browser data or uninstall the app. Regular backups are essential!
  • Use the export button regularly to backup your data
  • Enable backup reminders in Settings
  • Keep your exported files in a safe place

Where's My Backup?

Your backup file is named like: medtrack-backup-2026-02-16-14-30-45.json

On Android Phone

  1. Open the Files app (or "My Files" on Samsung)
  2. Tap Downloads folder
  3. Look for your medtrack-backup-*.json file
  4. Sort by "Date" to see the most recent files first
